Habits & Routines

I / YOU / WE / THEY

Use the base verb to talk about routines.

"I wake up at 6:59 AM."

HE / SHE / IT

Add "s" or "es" to the verb!

"He takes a walk in the morning."

Negatives & Questions

DON'T vs DOESN'T

Use don't for I/You/We/They. Use doesn't for He/She/It.

"I don't drink tea."
"The horse doesn't want my grass."

THE LIE DETECTOR

Tell the teacher 3 things about your daily routine. 2 are TRUE, 1 is a LIE!
